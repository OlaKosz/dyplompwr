\streszczenie{
A method for analysis of chromatograms is introduced. The method is based on
previously presented solutions and ideas. The method presented in this paper
separates complex chromatogram into single peaks and provides statistical tools
to choose and rate the best set of peaks. The method is examined on artificial
and real chromatograms. The obtained results are adequate and indicate good
performance of presented method.
}
